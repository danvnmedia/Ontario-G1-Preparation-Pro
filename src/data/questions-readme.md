# Ontario G1 Test - Question Database

This file contains the question database for the Ontario G1 Preparation Pro application.

## Categories

### Road Signs
- Warning Signs (Yellow diamond-shaped)
- Regulatory Signs (White rectangular or circular)
- Information Signs (Blue or green)
- Construction Signs (Orange)

### Traffic Rules
- Speed limits
- Right of way
- Parking regulations
- Highway rules

### Safety
- Seatbelt laws
- Impaired driving
- Distracted driving
- Emergency procedures

### G1 License Requirements
- Age requirements: Must be at least 16 years old
- Vision test required
- Knowledge test: 40 multiple-choice questions
- Passing grade: 80% on rules section, 80% on signs section
- Zero blood alcohol requirement
- Must be accompanied by fully licensed driver with at least 4 years experience
- Cannot drive between midnight and 5 AM
- Cannot drive on 400-series highways or high-speed roads
- No handheld devices allowed

## Question Format

Each question should include:
- Question text
- 4 multiple choice options
- Correct answer index (0-3)
- Detailed explanation
